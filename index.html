Our Core Mission

One Stream, Every Voice

Our mission is elegantly simple yet technologically ambitious: to empower any content creator to broadcast a single live stream that can be heard and understood by anyone, anywhere, in their native language.

Unified Global Community

Cultivate a worldwide audience without the logistical nightmare of managing separate streams or the prohibitive cost of human interpreters.

Authentic AI Voice

Our cutting-edge voice generation AI creates authentic, engaging voiceovers, ensuring your personality and emotion are not lost in translation.

Multi-Platform Sync

Translated audio tracks are synced with your video and broadcast simultaneously to platforms like YouTube and Twitch on different language channels.

The Technology of Connection

Go Global in Three Simple Steps

1. Connect

Securely link your streaming software and platform accounts (YouTube, Twitch, etc.) to the StreamVerse dashboard.

2. Select

Choose a portfolio of target languages for your broadcast from our extensive and ever-growing library.

3. Go Live

Begin your stream as you normally would. StreamVerse's engine takes over, processing and broadcasting in real-time.

The Architects

A Team Fueled by Passion

StreamVerse is not the product of a large corporation but of a nimble, dedicated team of ten university students in India. As avid consumers of global content and aspiring technologists, they have a firsthand understanding of the problem they are solving.

Their shared vision is to build a tool that democratizes global reach, giving creators from any background the power to connect with a worldwide audience. This unique position as both builders and members of the target audience gives them invaluable insight into creating a product that is not only powerful but also intuitive and creator-centric.
